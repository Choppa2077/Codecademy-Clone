Frequently asked questions

General

Whats the difference between Basic,Pro and Premium?

Basic gives you access to free courses and the ability to start some more advanced courses. Both Pro Lite and Pro include everything in Basic, plus extra content and features like real-world projects, guided paths, and assessments to test your skills. Pro is for people looking to learn or advance a skill — so it includes all courses and most skill paths. Premium is for those looking to start or advance their career — so it includes everything in Pro Lite, plus career paths and interview prep skill paths.

Do you offer a student discount?

Yes! Eligible college students can join Codecademy Pro Student. It’s an annual membership with all the same features as Codecademy Pro, but for over 35% off the regular price. Click here to join CodeStar Student.

Is CodeStar suitable for children?

Yes. Codecademy is suitable for children who can read and type. Coding is just like learning a new language or studying math. Children who learn to code when they’re young can set themselves up for a lifetime of opportunities for success.

Upgrades

Why should I upgrade from Basic

We built Codecademy Pro Lite and Pro based on the lessons we’ve learned from teaching over 45 million people to code since 2011. If you’re ready to accelerate your learning and apply your skills to real-world scenarios, either the Codecademy Pro Lite or Pro membership is for you.

How do I upgrade from Pro to Premium?

If you’re a current Pro Lite member and want to update to Pro, you can upgrade in the payment and plan section of your account settings.

Canceling

How do I cancel my subscription?

You may cancel your subscription at any time in your payment settings. When you cancel, your subscription ends at the end of the current billing cycle. You can also pause your subscription if you need to take a break.
